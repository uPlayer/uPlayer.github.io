uPlayer

uPlayer is an open source light-weight and straightforward music player for OS X .

A simple music player that only does music.

From a simple and minimalist interface, uPlayer is a command centre for your tunes.

Many of the usual features you’d expect from a good music player are present and correct – a play queue, an intelligent shuffle feature and custom key blindings.

Requires OS X 10.10 Yosemite
